⚠ PI & GE: High Rework Burden

BIM Models vs Design Drawings — Discipline Alignment Analysis

If drawings are extracted from BIM models, the model review outcome should lead or align with the design workflow outcome. Where design drawings are clearing review faster than BIM models, there is a coordination risk.

KEY FINDING In 14 of 19 disciplines, design drawings have a higher approval rate than their BIM models. The average gap is 18 pts. AR (+29 pts), WS (+31 pts) and SG (+32 pts) are the most misaligned — drawings ahead of models, suggesting drawings may not be fully model-derived.

Approval Rate: BIM Models vs Design Drawings (per discipline)

Green bars = BIM model approval rate. Blue bars = design workflow approval rate. Where blue exceeds green, designs are advancing ahead of their source models — a coordination risk. ID is the only fully aligned discipline (both 100%).

Approval Gap: Design Approval Rate MINUS BIM Approval Rate

Positive gap = designs are clearing review faster than BIM models. Negative = models ahead of drawings. A large positive gap suggests drawings may be produced without a fully approved model — a BIM protocol risk. SW (60 pts) and PL (71 pts) are statistical outliers with very small BIM sample sizes.

BIM Model Volume vs Design Workflow Volume (per discipline)

Comparing document counts. Some disciplines have far more BIM model documents than design workflows (e.g. WS: 197 BIM vs 85 Design), reflecting multi-format model deliverables (RVT, IFC, NWC) counted separately.

Rework Load: R&R Count — BIM vs Design

Revise & Resubmit burden comparison. ST has 148 BIM R&R and 154 Design R&R — aligned rework, suggesting consistent design-model integration. FP and WS BIM R&R counts significantly exceed their design R&R, indicating BIM models are iterating more than the extracted drawings.

Discipline-by-Discipline Alignment Cards

BIM approval % vs Design approval % with gap classification. Gap <10 pts = aligned; 10–25 pts = watch; >25 pts = misaligned risk.

🚨 AR, WS, SG: Designs Racing Ahead

Architectural (gap +29 pts), Water Supply (+31 pts) and Sewage (+32 pts) have designs clearing review at roughly double the rate of their BIM models. If drawings were derived from the model, the model should clear first — this sequence is inverted.

⚠ PI: Both BIM & Design Are Lagging

Process & Instrumentation has 15.2% BIM approval and 27.9% design approval — both among the lowest of any discipline. PI models and drawings are behind together. PI is a late-stage discipline; its delays cascade to commissioning packages.

🎉 ID: Perfectly Aligned at 100%

Interior Design is the only discipline where BIM models and design drawings are both 100% approved. Small sample size (10 BIM, 8 Design), but demonstrates what good BIM-to-drawing workflow integration looks like in practice.
